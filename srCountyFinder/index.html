Skip's County Finder - US West Coast states of Oregon, Washington, and California

An android app to help you locate nearby county boundaries.
Developed to assist birders with their county bird lists, including offshore pelagic trips.

⏩ Main Features:
✔️ Show ones current location in a county, and proximity to adjacent counties.
✔️ Works offline.  Designed to be functional when there is no cell signal.
✔️ Estimate the time and distance to the next county at the current speed and direction.
✔️ Offshore counties are defined using Closest Point of Land (CPOL), the same that eBird uses.
✔️ Allows one to keep a log of notes, tagged with date and location.
